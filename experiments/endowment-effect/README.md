# 🏺 The Endowment Effect

> 8 valuation scenarios in 4 hidden pairs across coffee mug (KKT 1990), NCAA Final Four ticket (Carmon & Ariely 2000 14-to-1 ratio), childhood book (Strahilevitz & Loewenstein 1998 ownership-duration), raffle ticket (Knetsch & Sinden 1984). Each item priced twice — once you own it, once you do not. WIZ measures your Endowment Gap against 40 years of WTA/WTP data.
